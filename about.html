Who We Are:

An electronic platform licensed by the Ministry of Justice in Syria, which was established in late 2020 to meet the market's need for a virtual auction that simulates auctions that are held on the ground, after a series of procedures that limited gathering and travel. "Mzadak" includes a group of experts specialized in the field of evaluating antique and rare collectibles and in the management of buying and selling operations, to be a safe bridge between the owners of valuable and heritage collectibles and those with high taste who are looking for excellence. What distinguishes Mzadak platform is the presence of an integrated legal team that is keen to complete all procedures from the moment the auction is opened until the completion of the ownership transfer process. We in "Mzadak" ensure you the best price, safety and speed of procedures.
